We design multi-step AI pipelines that can reason, retrieve, and execute across complex operational tasks with minimal human intervention. A typical build combines a reasoning model (Claude API) with retrieval (Pinecone or a vector store), workflow orchestration (n8n or LangChain), and targeted human-in-the-loop checkpoints where the risk profile demands it. We instrument everything — so you can see what the agent is doing, where it's uncertain, and where it's saving time. The goal is always systems that perform reliably in production, not just in controlled demos.
